About Me

Hello! My name is Jacob Vassas and I am a student in Cleveland, Ohio. I recently graduated from Cleveland State University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Health Sciences and a Minor in Business. I am also enrolled in Case Western Reserve's Coding Bootcamp.

My professional experience consists of working in the I.T. field at different levels. In 2018, I began interning at Mercy Regional Hospital in Lorain, Ohio in the Health Informatics department. Coinciding with working at Mercy, I have done work within my family's business, Vassas Telecommunications Consulting (VTC) since 2016. VTC specializes in voice and data audit analysis and voice, data and I.T. consulting.

When I am neither working, or studying, I bartend part-time in downtown Cleveland. My total experience consists of 3 years and my favorite cocktail is an Old Fashioned. This may seem to be a very hectic schedule but I believe that staying busy is extremely beneficial when striving towards goals. My interests include technology, large cities and urban areas, food, travel, and Cleveland sports.
